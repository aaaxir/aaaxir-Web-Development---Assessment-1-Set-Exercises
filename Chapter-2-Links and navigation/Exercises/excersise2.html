Plot

"Scarface" is a crime drama film directed by Brian De Palma, starring Al Pacino as Tony Montana, a Cuban immigrant who rises to power in the Miami drug scene. The movie follows Tony's journey as he builds his criminal empire, facing challenges from rival gangs and battling his own inner demons. Set against the backdrop of the 1980s, "Scarface" explores themes of ambition, greed, and the consequences of unchecked power.

Reviews

"Scarface" has garnered both praise and controversy since its release. With its iconic performances, memorable quotes, and intense action, it has become a cult classic in the crime genre.
